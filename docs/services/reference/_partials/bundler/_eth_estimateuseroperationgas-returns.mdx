**Type:** `Object`

An object containing the estimated gas values for the user operation.

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="v0.7" label="v0.7 and v0.8">

- __INLINE_CODE_1__: (string) - The amount of gas to pay for to compensate the bundler for
    pre-verification execution and __INLINE_CODE_2__.
- __INLINE_CODE_3__: (string) - The amount of gas to allocate for the verification step.
- __INLINE_CODE_4__: (string) - The amount of gas to allocate the main execution call.
- __INLINE_CODE_5__: (string) - The amount of gas to allocate for the verification step
    of the paymaster, or __INLINE_CODE_6__ if no paymaster.
- __INLINE_CODE_7__: (string) - The amount of gas to allocate for the post-operation step of
    the paymaster, or __INLINE_CODE_8__ if no paymaster.

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="v0.6" label="v0.6">

- __INLINE_CODE_9__: (string) - The amount of gas to pay for to compensate the bundler for
    pre-verification execution and __INLINE_CODE_10__.
- __INLINE_CODE_11__: (string) - The amount of gas used for verification (legacy field from v0.6).
- __INLINE_CODE_12__: (string) - The amount of gas to allocate for the verification step.
- __INLINE_CODE_13__: (string) - The amount of gas to allocate the main execution call.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
